Jungkook

Jeon Jung-kook, known as Jungkook, is a South Korean singer who rose to fame as a member and vocalist of the boy group BTS. He has released solo songs within BTS's discography and in 2022 featured in Charlie Puth's single "Left and Right". In 2023, he released his debut solo single "Seven" which topped multiple global charts.
